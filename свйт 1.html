Behind the Designs

I am a digital product designer specializing in creating high-quality, ready-to-use files for makers and creators. Whether it's precision-engineered models for 3D printers, intricate layered designs for laser cutting, or scalable vectors for plotters.

My workflow bridges the gap between digital imagination and physical fabrication. I optimize every node and mesh to ensure seamless results on Cricut, Silhouette, CNC lasers, and resin/FDM printers.
